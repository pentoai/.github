## We build AI that moves metrics, not slides.

Pento is a team of **Machine Learning and Software Engineers** who design, build, and deploy AI systems that solve real business problems. From semantic search and recommendation engines to computer vision and generative AI, we deliver end-to-end — from research and prototyping through production.

Our engineering team brings deep hands-on expertise across ML, full-stack development, and cloud infrastructure. We integrate directly into client teams and operate in North American time zones.

### Open Source

We contribute tools and research back to the community, with a focus on **AI-powered search** and **ML workflows**:

- **[vectory](https://github.com/pentoai/vectory)** — Track and compare embedding versions. Essential tooling for teams iterating on search and retrieval systems.
- **[ml-ralph](https://github.com/pentoai/ml-ralph)** — An autonomous ML research agent that runs experiments, maintains a living plan, and learns as it goes.
- **[x-unet](https://github.com/pentoai/x-unet)** — U-Net implementation with efficient attention, incorporating the latest research.
